# Code of Conduct
The kamp-us community is committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who wishes to contribute to the project. We value the diversity of our contributors and we strive to create a culture of respect, empathy, and understanding.

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by the following Code of Conduct. If you witness or experience any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers immediately.

## Expected Behavior
All participants in the kamp-us community are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate towards others.
- Use inclusive language and avoid language that is offensive, discriminatory, or derogatory.
- Listen actively to others and seek to understand their perspectives.
- Give credit where credit is due and respect intellectual property rights.
- Respect the privacy and personal information of others.

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are considered unacceptable in the kamp-us community:

- Harassment or intimidation of any kind, including but not limited to verbal, physical, or sexual harassment.
- Discrimination on the basis of race, ethnicity, nationality, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, religion, or any other personal characteristic.
- Bullying, trolling, or other forms of abusive behavior.
- Spamming, hacking, or other malicious activities.
- Any other behavior that is deemed inappropriate or offensive by the project maintainers.


## Reporting and Enforcement
If you witness or experience any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers immediately. All reports will be kept confidential and will be taken seriously.

The project maintainers reserve the right to take any action they deem appropriate in response to a violation of this Code of Conduct, including but not limited to warning the offender, removing the offender from the project, or banning the offender from future participation in the project.

## Thank You
Thank you for helping to create a welcoming and inclusive community around kamp-us. Your contributions are greatly appreciated!
